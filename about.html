About

Behind the lens of Wherever You Go Photography.

Hello, I'm Yugo Iwamoto.

I’m a travel and lifestyle photographer based in New York, capturing the beauty of everyday moments across the world. Through Wherever You Go Photography, I aim to tell stories of people, nature, and the subtle intersections of emotion and light that define our journeys.

My work is inspired by movement — both physical and emotional — and how our surroundings shape who we are. From quiet city mornings to the vibrant energy of distant landscapes, I believe every photograph carries a fragment of human experience worth remembering.

“Wherever you go, there’s a story waiting to be seen.”
